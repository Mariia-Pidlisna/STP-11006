About the Game

One day, a wild tornado swept across the world of Jolly.
It tore off roofs, tangled the roads, and scattered everything that was important to the inhabitants.
Now this world stands silent and a little lost — and it’s waiting just for you.

Along the way, you’ll meet quirky helpers: a comedian who laughs at the wrong things; a trickster who helps by accident; a loafer who does everything very slowly, but at just the right moment.
They’re imperfect, but truly yours
